A craft at risk.

In Pakistan's Baltistan region, centuries-old wool-weaving traditions are disappearing. Valuable wool is discarded as waste, and a sustainable culture faces extinction.

From Waste to Weave.

K2Knits transforms this challenge into an opportunity. We are building a decentralized circular economy—a dynamic digital platform that connects artisans, preserves chemical-free techniques, and creates a global marketplace for sustainable products.

[P]

Preserve

Creating living documentation of weaving techniques using Ceramic Network and Livepeer—a dynamic archive owned by the community.

[I]

Innovate

Tracking pattern evolution and enabling peer-to-peer knowledge sharing to ensure the craft evolves without losing its essence.

[C]

Connect

A decentralized marketplace gives artisans direct access to global consumers, ensuring fair compensation and cultural authenticity.
