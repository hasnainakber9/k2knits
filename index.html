A Craft at Risk.
A Resource Wasted.

In Pakistan's Baltistan region, centuries-old wool-weaving traditions face extinction. Valuable indigenous wool is often discarded, losing cultural heritage and a sustainable resource.

From Waste to Weave.

K2Knits transforms this challenge into opportunity. We're building a decentralized circular economy—a dynamic digital platform connecting artisans, preserving techniques, and creating a global marketplace.
